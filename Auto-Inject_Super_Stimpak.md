# Description
When your hit points are equal to or lower than half their maximum (rounded down) for the first time on a turn; you automatically gain the effects of a [[Super_Stimpak]].
# Summary
- Cost: 300c
- Load: 6
- Effect: heal = 2x healing rate (ghouls only heal 1x)
